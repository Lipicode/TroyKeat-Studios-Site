🗑️ Trasch

Welcome to Trasch! Hunt for amazing loot, sell it to the NPC, buy better tools, level up, and much more! Pull off a rewarding dumpster dive and become the best in the game! 🥇

How To Play

  • Go to a Dumpster and hold "E"!
  • Click all of the buttons until you are finished. (Follow the Label at the bottom!)
  • Take your Items and sell them to an NPC in your area for Cash!
  • Complete your Quests for each area!
  • Level up and unlock better places & tools!
